A West Coast IPA with Nectaron, Galaxy, Nelson Sauvin (Cryo) and Abstrax Omni Galaxy (extract)  hops.

**Name**: Nelson's Orbit<br />
**Style**: American IPA (21A)<br />
**Type**: All Grain

## Recipe Specifications

**Boil Size**: 7.0 gal (26.5L)<br />
**Batch Size (fermenter)**: 5.5 gal (20.8L)<br />
**Estimated OG**: 1.065<br />
**Estimated Color**: 5.1 SRM<br />
**Estimated IBU**: 54 (tinseth)<br />
**Estima.ed ABV**: 6.4%<br />
**Brewhouse Efficiency**: 70%<br />
**Boil Time**: 60 minutes<br />
